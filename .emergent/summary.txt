- **AWS Integration:** S3, SES, CloudFront, Lambda, Rekognition for scalable media operations.
- **Enhanced Upload:** Drag-and-drop media upload with comprehensive metadata input and real-time validation.
- **Phase 1: Metadata Parser & Validator:** Support for DDEX ERN (XML), MEAD, JSON, CSV with schema, field, and duplicate validation.
- **Phase 2: Advanced Metadata Features:** Batch processing, advanced reporting, and support for additional metadata formats (ID3, MusicBrainz, iTunes, ISNI).
- **Phase 3: Rights & Compliance Checker:** Checks for territory, usage rights, expiry dates, and embargo logic.
- **Phase 4: Smart Contract Trigger:** Auto-trigger licensing or DAO votes on valid content submission using Web3/blockchain integration (Ethereum mainnet).
- **Logging & Audit Trail:** Immutable logs of uploads, validations, rights checks, and timestamped metadata snapshots, with content owner, admin, and super admin access, export formats, and real-time notifications.
- **System Stability & Cleanup:** Achieve 100% functionality by fixing all errors and critical problems, and systematically remove all placeholder and 'emergent' references to prepare for production.
